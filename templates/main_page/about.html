Functions

Segmentation

Our advanced software leverages the power of OpenCV to automatically and accurately segment blood cell images, enabling precise identification and analysis.

Classification

Experience state-of-the-art classification capabilities as our software employs a fine-tuned VGG-16 model, providing unparalleled accuracy in identifying various blood cell types.

Active Learning

Harness the power of active learning with our web interface, allowing users to correct and refine wrong predicted labels.

Pipeline

  • Training with Weighted Sampler

    Our software initiates the journey by training on a labeled dataset of 111 samples. To address dataset imbalances, we employ a sophisticated weighted sampler that calculates class weights.

  • Uncertainty-Driven Predictions

    Step into the world of predictive certainty as our software generates accurate predictions. Leveraging the power of entropy, we employ a cutting-edge technique of 10 times forward propagation with random dropout (Monte-Carlo dropout) to provide robust and reliable predictions.

  • Insights Unveiled with Grad-CAM

    Unleash the potential of model explanation and visualization with Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping). Witness the inner workings of the model as we offer intuitive insights into its decision-making process, providing you with a deeper understanding of the predictions.

  • Empowering User Correction and Model Refinement

    Take the reins of model improvement into your hands with our web interface. Correct mispredicted data points, and through your inputs, fine-tune the model. Our software seamlessly integrates user-corrected labels, ensuring constant evolution and enhanced accuracy.
